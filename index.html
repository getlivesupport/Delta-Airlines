How to Cancel a Delta Airlines Flight and Get Your Money Back

Follow these exact steps. If any step fails, call 1(856)900-7834 — a live agent handles it in minutes.

  1. Check Your Ticket Type First

    Open your Delta booking confirmation email. Find the fare class — Refundable, Non-Refundable, Basic Economy, or Comfort+. Your refund eligibility depends entirely on this. Refundable = full cash refund. Non-Refundable = eCredit. Basic Economy = no refund after 24 hours.

  2. Go to My Trips on Delta.com

    Visit delta.com and click "My Trips" in the header. Enter your 6-digit confirmation number and last name exactly as it appears on your ticket. Or log into your SkyMiles account to see all bookings.

    Within 24 hours of booking? Cancel immediately for a guaranteed full refund — no questions asked, all ticket types.

  3. Select the Flight and Click "Cancel Flight"

    Click on your booking, then select the specific flight segment you want to cancel. Click "Cancel Flight." If you're cancelling a round trip, decide whether to cancel one direction or both — they process separately.

  4. Choose Refund to Original Payment (Not eCredit)

    When prompted, choose "Refund to original payment method" if your ticket is refundable. Don't accept an eCredit if you're entitled to cash. If Delta pushes eCredit instead of cash, call immediately — you have the right to a cash refund on cancelled flights.

    If Delta cancelled your flight, you're entitled to a full cash refund regardless of ticket type.

  5. Confirm and Save Your Cancellation Number

    Submit the cancellation and save the confirmation number from your email. Credit card refunds arrive within 7 business days. Cash refunds within 20 business days. Screenshot the refund confirmation as proof.

  6. Refund Overdue or Website Failing? Call Right Now

    If your refund hasn't arrived after 7 business days, the website shows an error, or a live agent is required to override a system block — don't wait longer.

    Call now: 1(856)900-7834 — a live Delta specialist resolves refund escalations directly.
