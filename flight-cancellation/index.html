Most Important Rule

Delta Airlines 24-Hour Cancellation Policy — The Rule That Protects Every Passenger

This is the most powerful cancellation right you have — and Delta must honor it by law.

You Have 24 Hours — Use Them

The DOT (Department of Transportation) requires all US airlines including Delta to offer a full refund within 24 hours of booking, as long as the flight departs at least 7 days later. This applies to ALL ticket types including Basic Economy. Delta cannot charge you any fee. This is federal law — not a Delta policy they can change.

  1. When Does the 24-Hour Clock Start?

    The 24 hours begins the moment you complete your booking — when you receive the confirmation email. It does not start from when you discover a mistake or when a third-party travel agent confirms the booking.

    If your flight is less than 7 days away, the 24-hour rule does NOT apply — but you may still cancel under your ticket's normal rules.

  2. How to Cancel Within 24 Hours Online

    Go to delta.com → My Trips → enter confirmation number → select Cancel Flight. The system will automatically offer a full refund when you're within the 24-hour window. Choose "Refund to original payment method."

  3. What If the Website Won't Give You the Refund?

    If delta.com shows an error or only offers an eCredit during your 24-hour window, call immediately. Do not let the 24 hours expire while troubleshooting the website.

    Call 1(856)900-7834 — a live agent can process your 24-hour refund directly, bypassing the website.
Step-by-Step

How to Cancel a Delta Airlines Flight Online — Full Process

Exact steps from start to refund confirmation. If any step fails, call 1(856)900-7834.

  1. Find Your Confirmation Number and Ticket Type

    Open your Delta booking email. Your 6-character confirmation code is at the top. Also note your fare class — Refundable, Non-Refundable, or Basic Economy. This determines exactly what you'll get back.

  2. Go to delta.com and Click "My Trips"

    Visit delta.com and select "My Trips" at the top of the page. Enter your 6-digit confirmation code and last name exactly as on your ticket. Or log into your SkyMiles account to see all bookings in one place.

    Booked through Expedia, Google Flights or another agency? You may need to cancel there, not on delta.com. Call 1(856)900-7834 to confirm your cancellation path.

  3. Select Your Flight and Click "Cancel Flight"

    Click on your booking. If it's a multi-leg trip, choose the specific segment. Click "Cancel Flight." A summary will show your ticket type, refund eligibility, and refund amount before you confirm anything.

  4. Choose "Refund to Original Payment" — Not eCredit

    When prompted, always select "Refund to original payment method" if your ticket qualifies. Delta sometimes defaults to eCredit — override this if you want your money back in cash. Never accept eCredit if Delta cancelled your flight.

    If Delta cancelled your flight, choose CASH REFUND — not an eCredit. You have a legal right to cash under DOT rules.

  5. Confirm Cancellation and Save the Confirmation

    Click Confirm. You'll receive a cancellation email with a reference number. Screenshot it. Your refund should appear on your card in 7 business days. eCredits appear in your SkyMiles account within minutes.

  6. Refund Not Arriving or Website Failing? Call Now

    If 7 business days have passed with no refund, the portal is blocking your cancellation, or Delta is pushing eCredit when you're entitled to cash — don't wait. A live agent can escalate directly.

    Call now: 1(856)900-7834 — live Delta specialist overrides system blocks and processes refunds directly.
